## Main Idea

Observer Patch Holography (OPH) proposes that all of physics — spacetime structure, gauge symmetry, the Standard Model particle spectrum, and gravity — emerges as consequences of observer-overlap consistency on a finite-capacity holographic screen. Physics is local, patchwise, and whatever counts as reality must survive agreement across observer overlaps.

- **Only 2 input constants required** — most previously unexplained properties of the Universe, including the entire particle spectrum, derive from just one (P = a_cell/ℓ_P²)
- **3+1D Lorentzian spacetime** emerges automatically: S² screen's conformal group is SO⁺(3,1)
- **Einstein gravity** at large scales via modular flow, generalized entropy, and overlap consistency (Jacobson-style derivation)
- **Standard Model gauge group** SU(3) × SU(2) × U(1) / Z₆ reconstructed from patch gluing and charge-composition rules
- **Massless carriers**: photon, gluons, graviton emerge as structural zeros

- **Measurement problem**: observers and records built in from the start; Born probabilities arise from consistency conditions
- **Strange loop hypothesis**: reality as self-referential timeless causal structure closing on itself

## Method / Approach

OPH starts with:
1. Finite-capacity holographic screen (S² topology)
2. Local patch algebras with finite capacity
3. Overlap observables between neighboring patches
4. Refinement and generalized entropy
5. Minimal Admissible Realization principle (nature's Occam's razor)

Then asks: what physics is forced by requiring all patches fit together consistently?
